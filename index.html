Our Interactive Design Process

1

Consultation

Understanding your needs, space, and vision

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your educational goals, space constraints, budget, and design preferences. Our team visits your facility or conducts virtual meetings to assess your needs.

2

3D Design

Creating detailed renderings and concepts

Our designers create detailed 3D renderings and technical drawings, allowing you to visualize the final product before production begins. We provide multiple design iterations based on your feedback.

3

Prototyping

Testing and refining the design

We create prototypes or samples for complex pieces, allowing for physical testing and final adjustments. This ensures the furniture meets all safety standards and functional requirements.

4

Production

Manufacturing your custom pieces

Our skilled craftspeople manufacture your custom furniture using premium materials and precise techniques. We provide regular updates on production progress and delivery timelines.
